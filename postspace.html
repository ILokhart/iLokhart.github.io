The Crab Shack (Tybee Island, GA)

The crab shack is an indoor/outdoor seafood schmorgesborg located right on the water. It has great music, atmosphere, service, and food! It's also home to several rehabilitating alligators that you can view and feed. The gift shop will have something you can take home with you to remember the experience.

Ruby Falls (Chattanooga, TN)

Ruby falls is a spectacular cave featuring an underground waterfall that is 145 feet tall! Other local caves are great locations to explore alien landscapes and relax in the cool underground air. Find geological oddities, rare mineral deposits, underground lakes, and witness exotic wildlife.

Tallulah Gorge (Tallulah Falls, GA)

Immerse yourself in awe-inspiring beauty or daring adventures with a glimpse into the past at Tallulah Gorge State Park, where the Tallulah River flows through rugged terrain to carve a complex 1,000 foot geologic formation and five spectacular waterfalls which provide habitat for many unique species.
